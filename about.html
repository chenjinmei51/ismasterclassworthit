About Us

Our Mission

Welcome to IsMasterClassWorthIt.com — a place where real people share real experiences with MasterClass. Our mission is simple: to provide honest, in-depth reviews and guides to help you decide if MasterClass is right for you and your family.

Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Robert Larkin, the creator of this site. I'm a father, lifelong learner, and someone who was once skeptical about MasterClass. Like many of you, I wondered: Is MasterClass really worth the money?

That question led me to try MasterClass myself — and I was surprised by how much value I found. From learning to cook with Gordon Ramsay to fixing my sleep with Matthew Walker, MasterClass has been an unexpected but rewarding journey. Even my son got in on the learning by improving his basketball skills with Stephen Curry!

Why This Website?

When I first looked into MasterClass, I found it hard to get real, honest opinions. Everything felt like it was written by marketers, not actual users. That's why I created this website — to share my personal experiences, give you honest reviews, and help answer the big question: Is MasterClass worth it?

You'll find:

  • Detailed MasterClass reviews
  • Comparisons of different subscription plans
  • Best course recommendations for beginners
  • Tips on how to get the most out of your MasterClass membership
  • And much more!

Our Promise

Honesty First. All the content here is based on real experience. We only recommend what we've tried and what we think will genuinely help you.

No Fluff. No Fake Reviews. We're here to save you time and money — and hopefully inspire you to learn something new.
